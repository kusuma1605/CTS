Sprint 1 is 10 working days. Sketch (on paper or in a spreadsheet) an
*ideal* burndown line from 13 points remaining on Day 0 to 0 points
remaining on Day 10, then plot a plausible *actual* burndown where the

## Step 4 - Retrospective questions
At the "end" of this simulated sprint, answer:
1. What would you flag in Sprint Review as done vs. not-done, and why?
2. What's one thing that slowed the team down (the blocked story) and one
   concrete action to prevent it next sprint?
3. Which Scrum ceremony is responsible for surfacing that blocker as early
   as possible in a real sprint?
